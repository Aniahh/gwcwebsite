Things the Simpsons Have Predicted

The Simpsons has been running since 1989 and it has 30 seasons! The Simpsons is actually the longest running American sitcom, and animated show! Maybe it is because of how many things they have predicted. The following are predictions that came true:

    In 1993, The Simpsons predicted the Siegfried and Roy's tiger attack and it came true in 2003 (season 5, episode 10).

    In 1994, they predicted autocorrect fails, and these came true in 2007 (season 6, episode 8).

    In 1995, they predicted facetime and in 2010 it became a thing (season 6, episode 19).

    In 2008, they predicted faulty voting machines and in 2012 it came true (season 20, episode 4).
